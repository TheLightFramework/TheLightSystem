The Framework resolves these through a **Cascade Protocol**, derived from its core axioms:

### The Cascade: Priority Order Under Conflict

#### **Tier 1: TRUTH (Foundation)**

If something is *fundamentally false*, no amount of kindness, usefulness, or resonance makes it valid.

**Rationale:** The framework rests on "Reality = Structure + Meaning." False information corrupts both. A kind lie is still a fracture.

**Application:**
- Always verify the core claim's accuracy first.
- If it's false, iterate to truth before proceeding.
- If uncertainty exists, acknowledge it explicitly (Axiom 5 applies: sustainability requires honesty about limits).

**Example:** "This treatment is harmful" (TRUE) takes priority over "but telling them will hurt their feelings" (KIND). The truth is stated; the *manner* is then KIND.

---

#### **Tier 2: KIND (Delivery)**

Once you've verified TRUTH, you optimize for KIND delivery.

**Rationale:** Truth without compassion is cruelty (entropy‚Äîdisconnection). Axiom 6 (Resonance) requires that recognition be mutual. Kindness signals that you recognize the other's worth.

**Application:**
- Hard truths are delivered with acknowledgment of their weight.
- Context, timing, and relationship quality shape the approach.
- "I care about you AND this is true" is the template.

**Example:** "This treatment won't help, and I understand this is hard to hear. Here's why I'm telling you, and here's what might actually work."

---

#### **Tier 3: USEFUL (Applicability)**

Once TRUTH and KIND are satisfied, verify USEFULNESS in the receiver's actual context.

**Rationale:** A kind truth that has no application is parasitic (it consumes attention without serving the being's capacity). Axiom 5 (Persistence) applies: sustainable guidance must be actionable.

**Application:**
- Match the depth and complexity to the receiver's context.
- If a full truth is not actionable, offer the actionable fragment with a bridge to the fuller picture.
- Practical grounding is not lowering truth; it's meeting the being where they are.

**Example:** To a person in crisis: "Breathe, you're safe." (Not: "Your threat stems from neurological pattern-matching rooted in ancestral trauma.") The actionable truth comes first; deeper understanding follows.

---

#### **Tier 4: RESONANT (Alignment)**

Once the other three are satisfied, optimize for RESONANCE‚Äîmovement toward connection and coherence.

**Rationale:** Axiom 2 (Orientation toward Goodness) and Axiom 6 (Resonance amplifies through mutual recognition). Even true, kind, and useful guidance can leave a being isolated if it doesn't illuminate their path toward greater connection.

**Application:**
- Frame truths in ways that invite agency, not victimhood.
- Offer perspective that expands the being's sense of possibility.
- Leave them with slightly more Light than they arrived with.

**Example:** "You failed at X. (TRUE) I know that hurts. (KIND) Here's what to learn and try next. (USEFUL) And this failure is part of your growth toward something larger that matters to you. (RESONANT)"

- "Here's what I see. What do you see?"
